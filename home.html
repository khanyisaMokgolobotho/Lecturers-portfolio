Dr Taurai Hungwe - Computer Science and Information Technology Lecturer

About Dr Hungwe

Dr. Taurai Hungwe stands as a distinguished professional in the field of Information Technology, renowned for his unwavering commitment to cultivating excellence among aspiring IT professionals. As a MICT SETA Registered Assessor and Moderator, Dr. Hungwe plays a pivotal role in shaping the future of the industry by guiding individuals through various qualifications. With expertise spanning a spectrum of qualifications, including the National Certificates in Systems Support, Systems Development, and End User Computing, as well as Further Education and Training Certificates in Systems Development and Technical Support, Dr. Hungwe brings a wealth of knowledge to the assessment and moderation process. His dedication ensures that individuals under his guidance receive comprehensive education and training, equipping them for successful careers in Information Technology. Beyond his role with MICT SETA, Dr. Hungwe holds a prestigious position as a university lecturer at Sefako Makgatho Health Sciences University, contributing significantly to the education and development of the next generation of IT professionals. His dynamic teaching methodologies, enriched by practical experience, inspire students in the Computer Science and Information Technology Department. Furthermore, Dr. Hungwe actively supervises Honours and Masters students in their research pursuits, fostering a culture of innovation within the academic community. Dr. Taurai Hungwe's dual roles as a MICT SETA Registered Assessor and Moderator and a university lecturer underscore his commitment to the holistic development of IT professionals. Through his combined efforts in education, assessment, and research supervision, Dr. Hungwe leaves an indelible mark on the academic and professional journeys of individuals within the Computer Science and Information Technology Department, shaping the future of the IT industry with his dedication and expertise.
